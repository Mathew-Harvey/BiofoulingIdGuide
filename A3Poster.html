HIGH
Carpet Sea Squirt
Didemnum vexillum
Sea Squirt
  • Creamy/tan like old wax
  • Veiny like varicose veins
  • Leathery, NOT slimy
  • Drips like candle wax
HIGH
Asian Green Mussel
Perna viridis
Mussel
  • Bright green shell (young)
  • Brownish-green (older)
  • Big: 80-165mm
  • Blue-green inside
HIGH
European Fan Worm
Sabella spallanzanii
Tube Worm
  • HUGE spiral fan: 45-60mm
  • Stripy orange/purple/white
  • Brown leathery tube
  • Fan pulls in when close
HIGH
Black-striped False Mussel
Mytilopsis sallei
Mussel
  • Small: only 25mm
  • Dark stripes on shell
  • Likes brackish water
  • Check sea chests!
HIGH
White Colonial Sea Squirt
Didemnum perlucidum
Sea Squirt
  • White/see-through
  • Thin sheet on surface
  • Smooth texture
  • Already in WA!
HIGH
Killer Algae
Caulerpa taxifolia
Algae
  • Bright green - stands out
  • Feather-shaped fronds
  • Creeping runners
  • Up to 65cm tall
MED
Stalked Sea Squirt
Styela clava
Sea Squirt
  • Brown pickle on a stalk
  • Leathery & wrinkly
  • 50-160mm tall
  • Stands alone

Brown Mussel

Japanese Kelp (Wakame)
