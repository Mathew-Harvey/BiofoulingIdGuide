HIGH
Carpet Sea Squirt
Didemnum vexillum
Ascidian
  • Off-white to cream/tan/orange
  • Veiny, visible water channels
  • Smooth, leathery (not slimy)
  • Dripping 'wax-like' tendrils
HIGH
Asian Green Mussel
Perna viridis
Mussel
  • Bright green shell (juveniles)
  • Brownish-green (adults)
  • 80-165mm length
  • Blue-green interior
HIGH
Northern Pacific Seastar
Asterias amurensis
Seastar
  • Five arms, pointed upturned tips
  • Yellow/orange, purple markings
  • Up to 50cm diameter
  • Yellow underneath
HIGH
European Fan Worm
Sabella spallanzanii
Polychaete
  • Large spiral fan crown
  • Banded orange/purple/white
  • Brown leathery tube
  • Fan retracts when disturbed
HIGH
Black-striped False Mussel
Mytilopsis sallei
Bivalve
  • Small size (up to 25mm)
  • Dark stripes on shell
  • Tolerates brackish water
  • Forms dense colonies
HIGH
Zebra Mussel
Dreissena polymorpha
Bivalve
  • Zebra-like striped pattern
  • D-shaped shell
  • Up to 50mm length
  • Freshwater/brackish
HIGH
Quagga Mussel
Dreissena bugensis
Bivalve
  • Rounder than zebra mussel
  • Pale bands/stripes
  • Ventral side convex
  • Up to 40mm length
HIGH
White Colonial Sea Squirt
Didemnum perlucidum
Ascidian
  • White/translucent colonies
  • Thin encrusting sheets
  • Small zooids visible
  • Smooth surface texture
HIGH
Chinese Mitten Crab
Eriocheir sinensis
Crab
  • Dense fur on claws ("mittens")
  • Olive-brown carapace
  • Up to 80mm carapace width
  • Notched forehead
HIGH
Harris Mud Crab
Rhithropanopeus harrisii
Crab
  • Small size (up to 25mm)
  • Olive-brown colour
  • White-tipped claws
  • Three spines behind eyes
HIGH
Comb Jelly
Mnemiopsis leidyi
Comb Jelly
  • Transparent gelatinous body
  • Eight rows of cilia (combs)
  • Up to 120mm length
  • Iridescent in light
HIGH
Feather Algae / Killer Algae
Caulerpa taxifolia
Macroalga
  • Bright green feather fronds
  • Up to 65cm height
  • Creeping stolons
  • Dense meadow formation
MED
Stalked Sea Squirt
Styela clava
Ascidian
  • Club-shaped body on stalk
  • Brown to tan colour
  • Leathery, wrinkled texture
  • 50-160mm length
MED
European Shore Crab
Carcinus maenas
Crab
  • Green carapace
  • Five spines each side of eyes
  • Up to 90mm carapace width
  • Orange underneath
MED
Japanese Kelp / Wakame
Undaria pinnatifida
Macroalga
  • Large brown fronds + midrib
  • Wavy/ruffled edges
  • Up to 3m length
  • Sporophyll at base
MED
Vase Tunicate
Ciona intestinalis
Ascidian
  • Cylindrical vase shape
  • Translucent yellowish-green
  • Two visible siphons
  • Soft body, up to 150mm
MED
Brown Mussel
Perna perna
Mussel
  • Dark brown to black shell
  • Up to 90mm length
  • Forms dense clusters
  • Heavy hull fouler
MED
NZ Green-lipped Mussel
Perna canaliculus
Mussel
  • Green lip along shell margin
  • Brown-green shell exterior
  • Up to 240mm length
  • Distinctive periostracum
MED
Variable Mussel
Brachidontes pharaonis
Mussel
  • Variable colouration
  • Ribbed shell surface
  • Up to 35mm length
  • Intertidal/subtidal
MED
Basket Clam
Varicorbula gibba
Bivalve
  • Small white shells
  • Up to 12mm length
  • Asymmetric valves
  • Lives in soft sediment
MED
Pacific Oyster
Magallana gigas
Bivalve
  • Large irregular shell
  • Up to 400mm length
  • Rough fluted surface
  • Cements to surfaces
MED
Pleated Sea Squirt
Styela plicata
Ascidian
  • Pleated/ridged surface
  • Grey-brown colour
  • Up to 70mm height
  • Solitary, not colonial
MED
Asian Paddle Crab
Charybdis japonica
Crab
  • Paddle-shaped rear legs
  • Six spines between eyes
  • Up to 120mm carapace
  • Olive to brown colour
MED
Hook Weed
Bonnemaisonia hamifera
Macroalga
  • Red/pink branching fronds
  • Hook-like branchlets
  • Up to 30cm height
  • Pompom-like stage
